## üé¨ ANIMATION STATES

### Idle States (at desk)
- **Hunter:** Leaning back reviewing charts, occasional pointing at screen
- **Forge:** Rapid typing with occasional pause to think/drink energy drink
- **Echo:** Sketching on tablet with animated hand movements
- **Atlas:** Methodically clicking through organized workflows
- **Sentinel:** Reading documents with magnifying glass, making notes

### Walking (4 directions: N/S/E/W)
- **2-frame walk cycle** for each direction
- **Hunter:** Confident stride, briefcase swaying
- **Forge:** Casual shuffle, hands in hoodie pockets
- **Echo:** Bouncy step with animated gesturing
- **Atlas:** Professional pace, upright posture
- **Sentinel:** Thoughtful walk, hands behind back

### Meeting States
- **Sitting around conference table**
- **Speech bubbles** with character-specific icons:
  - Hunter: Dollar signs, upward arrows
  - Forge: Code brackets, gear icons
  - Echo: Lightbulbs, color swatches
  - Atlas: Checkmarks, flow charts
  - Sentinel: Question marks, magnifying glass

### Thinking States
- **Hunter:** Power stance with hand on chin
- **Forge:** Head scratching, glasses adjustment
- **Echo:** Pacing with wild gesturing
- **Atlas:** Steepled fingers contemplation
- **Sentinel:** Classic detective pose with pipe (but coffee mug)

### Celebration States
- **Hunter:** Fist pump with "YES!" energy
- **Forge:** Subtle satisfied nod with slight smile
- **Echo:** Jazz hands with sparkling effects
- **Atlas:** Professional handshake gesture
- **Sentinel:** Approving nod with slight smile
